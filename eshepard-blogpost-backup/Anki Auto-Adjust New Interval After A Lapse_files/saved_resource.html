                        Ed Mod Cacambo • 7 months ago

                        You seem to have written Ivl (capitol i; v, lower-case L) as a separate term or something in the code.

                        change:
                        where bLastIvl < aLastIvl
                        limit %s) as o
                        where ease > 1""" % lapsed_rev_records

                        to :
                        where bLastlvl < aLastlvl ) as o
                        where ease > 1"""

                        Note: bLastlvl and aLastlvl end in lower-case-L, v, lower-case-L. There can be no spaces between them.
